Safety & Trust

Platform vision only works if the base feels safe.

MAGSNAP should answer vision, weight, magnet, battery, and module compatibility concerns directly. Confidence beats hype.

Will MAGSNAP make me cross-eyed?

MAGSNAP does not place a prism, display, or lens in front of your eyes, and you do not look at the camera while using it. The camera is designed to sit outside your normal line of sight. If you experience eye strain, double vision, headache, dizziness, or blocked vision, stop using it immediately.

Can I attach any module?

No. Use only tested lightweight modules. The platform is magnetic, but compatibility, weight, field of view, heat, and safety still matter.

Does the base eyewear contain electronics?

No. The base glasses contain no battery, processor, built-in camera, microphone, speaker, or display. Electronics live in removable modules.

What about the Light Module battery?

The Light Module should be treated as a real electronic module, not a free gift. Battery, charging, heat, and shipping requirements must be tested before full rollout.
