Origin

From daily use to a magnetic platform.

MZ worked in DJI Europe sales and project management. MAGSNAP grew from daily product use, field testing, and a simple question: what if the eyewear base stayed simple while modules kept changing?

Safety notes: MAGSNAP is not protective eyewear, medical equipment, or a promise of safe use in every activity. No prism, display, or lens sits in front of the eye; modules should sit outside normal line of sight. Stop use if eye strain, double vision, headache, dizziness, blocked vision, or discomfort occurs. Use only tested lightweight modules and mounting positions. Do not attach heavy, sharp, hot, or unverified objects. Keep magnets away from pacemakers, implanted medical devices, magnetic cards, and sensitive electronics. The base glasses contain no battery; battery modules must be tested separately from the eyewear base. Do not use where distraction, impact, blocked vision, heat, water, or module failure could create risk.
