04

Frames

The frame is part of the system.

The large frame, wide temples, and horizontal top line are deliberate product decisions.

Large frontMore visual coverage and a stronger base for outdoor use.
Wide templesDesigned with wind and sand in mind.
Flat top lineBetter compatibility with helmets and head-worn gear.
Module zonePositioned outside the normal line of sight.
