Safety / Warning

Use it like real gear.

Early builder hardware needs clear limits.

Vision FirstNo prism, display, or lens sits in front of your eye. Stop use if you feel eye strain, double vision, headache, dizziness, or blocked vision.
Module LimitsUse only tested lightweight modules and mounting positions. Do not attach heavy, sharp, hot, or unverified objects.
Magnet WarningKeep magnets away from pacemakers, implanted medical devices, magnetic cards, and sensitive electronics.
Battery ModulesThe base glasses contain no battery. Battery safety for removable modules, including the Light Module, must be tested separately.
MAGSNAP is not protective eyewear, medical equipment, or a promise of safe use in every activity. Do not use it where distraction, impact, blocked vision, heat, water, or module failure could create risk.
