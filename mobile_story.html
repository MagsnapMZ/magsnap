How it works

Wear. Snap. Switch.

The full explanation should fit into three moves.

Wear Put on the eyewear base. No battery or processor is built into the base glasses.
Snap Attach a tested lightweight module to the magnetic interface.

Design intelligence

Built for real use.

The shape is not decoration. The frame, temples, top line, and module position all carry design intent.

Large frameDesigned with more visual coverage and a stronger physical presence.
Wide templesBuilt with wind, sand, and outdoor use in mind.
Flat top lineBetter fit with helmets and head-worn gear.
Module positionThe module sits outside the normal line of sight.

Safety notes

Use only tested lightweight modules.

MAGSNAP is not protective eyewear, medical equipment, or a promise of safe use in every activity.

VisionStop use if you feel eye strain, double vision, headache, dizziness, or blocked vision.
MagnetsKeep magnets away from pacemakers, implanted medical devices, cards, and sensitive electronics.
ModulesDo not attach heavy, sharp, hot, or unverified objects.
BatteryThe base has no battery. Battery modules must be tested separately.
