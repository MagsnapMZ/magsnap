Lens Improvement
镜片升级计划

The first generation of MagSnap is not perfect.

Among all components, the lens is the area we are least satisfied with.

Finding the right lens has been one of the hardest parts of this project.

  • Large lens size
  • Saltwater resistance
  • Optical clarity
  • Polarization
  • Durability

If you know lens factories, optical engineers, lens suppliers, or better lens solutions, we would love to hear from you.

Help us build the next generation of MagSnap.

第一代 MagSnap 并不完美。

在所有零件里，镜片是我们目前最不满意的部分。

为了找到适合海水环境的大尺寸镜片，我们测试和对比了很多工厂。

Product Feedback / Quality Improvement

MagSnap V2.0 产品优化方向
V2.0 Optimization Direction

基于真实运动场景和用户反馈，MagSnap 正在持续优化镜片性能、磁吸兼容性和佩戴舒适度。以下内容属于测试反馈与后续优化方向，不代表当前量产配置。
Based on real-world testing and user feedback, MagSnap is improving comfort, compatibility and performance. These are V2.0 optimization directions under testing, not current production features.

Under Testing / 后续优化

海上运动镜片测试
Ocean Sports Lens Testing

正在评估新的运动镜片供应链与测试方案，包括偏光、防紫外线、抗冲击、耐海水和长期户外使用表现。后续会重点验证 PC / Nylon 偏光方案、盐雾测试、镀膜与边缘封装，以及灰片、蓝膜、铜片、高透片等样品。

We are evaluating advanced polarized sports lenses for saltwater resistance, UV protection, impact resistance and long-term outdoor durability.

Future Design / V2.0 方向

双磁极兼容设计
Dual Polarity Magnetic Compatibility

测试中发现，部分第三方磁吸设备因磁极方向不同，可能出现吸附不稳或排斥。未来版本正在评估左右镜腿采用不同磁极方向，让用户根据设备选择更适合的一侧安装。

Future versions may test opposite polarity layouts on the left and right temples to support more third-party magnetic accessories.

Fit Improvement / 舒适度优化

多高度鼻托系统
Multi-Height Nose Pad System

不同用户鼻梁高度和脸型差异明显。MagSnap 正在评估 Standard / High，未来也可能扩展为 Low / Standard / High 的可更换鼻托，以提升舒适度、稳定性和运动适配。

We are exploring replaceable nose pads in multiple heights to improve fit, comfort and stability during sports.
