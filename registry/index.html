MAGSNAP REGISTRY

MAGSNAP REGISTRY is the identity layer for the first co-creators and early field-test records of MagSnap's Extreme Technology Platform.

MagSnap is not an eyewear company. Eyewear is only the first product; the Registry records the people and tests that help build what does not exist yet.

Registry Numbers come from physical QC/Test Cards. They do not follow registration order, purchase order, or purchase date.

Record activation is currently handled by MAGSNAP directly. If your card number is not listed, email info@magsnap.me with your card number and contact details.
