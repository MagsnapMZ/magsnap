# MAGSNAP Website Redesign Brief

Date: 2026-06-05

Status: historical brief. Current requirements live in `MAGSNAP_SOURCE_OF_TRUTH.md`.

Important: this document contains older Founding 900, signup, and CTA ideas that are now deprecated for the current homepage. Do not use this file as the final source of truth without checking `MAGSNAP_SOURCE_OF_TRUTH.md`.

## New Website Objective

The website must reposition MAGSNAP from:

> Magnetic POV glasses

to:

> The first wearable magnetic ecosystem.

But the site must not become abstract. The first sellable use case remains:

> Lightweight action camera POV + magnetic Light Module.

This creates a two-layer message:

- Strategic layer: Wearable Magnetic Ecosystem.
- Sales layer: Camera in the morning. Light at night.

## Core Website Message

### Primary Hero Line

> Not smart glasses. A wearable magnetic platform.

### Supporting Line

> Camera in the morning. Light at night. One base platform. Snap on what you need.

### Product Definition

> MAGSNAP is a lightweight eyewear base with a standardized magnetic interface for snap-on modules, starting with cameras and lights.

## Homepage Structure

### 1. Hero

Goal: Immediately break the “just glasses” frame.

Content:

- Not smart glasses.
- Wearable magnetic platform.
- Camera + Light as first two modules.
- Founder-used for over a year.
- CTA: Join Founding 900 / Watch the 30-second demo.

Visual:

- Large product image.
- Floating module cards:
  - Camera Module.
  - Light Module.
  - AI Pod, next.

### 2. Problem

Goal: Make users feel the mounting chaos.

Copy angle:

> Every device wants a different strap, clip, mount, or helmet. MAGSNAP gives your head one clean magnetic interface.

Devices:

- Action cameras.
- Microphones.
- Walkie talkies.
- Lights.
- Trackers.
- AI pods.

### 3. Platform Philosophy

Goal: Explain why base glasses have no electronics.

Copy:

> 100% magnetic. 0% electric in the base glasses.

Bullets:

- No battery.
- No processor.
- No built-in camera.
- No speaker.
- No display.

Result:

- Lightweight.
- Repairable.
- Upgradeable.
- Future-proof.

### 4. Founding 900 Module Kit

Goal: Sell the first batch as an ecosystem kit, not one pair of glasses.

Bundle:

- MAGSNAP Base Eyewear.
- Camera Mount Module.
- Magnetic Light Module.
- Founding 900 numbered card.

Important wording:

- Do not say “free headlight.”
- Say “Includes MAGSNAP Light Module.”

### 5. How It Works

Steps:

1. Wear the base platform.
2. Snap on the module.
3. Switch when the moment changes.

Micro-copy:

> Morning ride: camera. Night walk: light. Travel: AI pod. Same base.

### 6. Launch Modules

Current / near-term modules:

- Camera Module: Insta360 GO, DJI Osmo Nano, thumb cameras.
- Light Module: night walking, camp, repair, low-risk riding.
- Audio Module: DJI Mic and wireless microphones.
- AI Pod Module: concept validated, software not mature yet.

The page should clearly label what is ready vs in testing.

### 7. Field Proof

Goal: Make the site feel real.

Proof cards:

- Founder worn for over one year.
- Kitesurf and FPV test network.
- Mui Ne / Phan Rang clubs.
- 30-day first user tests.
- Visual safety testing in progress.

### 8. Safety & Trust

Goal: Answer American user concerns before they bounce.

Must include:

- Not a prism, display, or optical device that asks you to look at the camera.
- Camera sits outside normal line of sight.
- Stop use if eye strain, double vision, headache, dizziness, or blocked vision occurs.
- Use only tested lightweight modules.
- Base glasses contain no battery.
- Light Module battery safety under test.

### 9. Developer / Partner Teaser

Goal: Open the ecosystem door without overpromising.

Copy:

> The long-term asset is the magnetic interface. If you build small wearable modules for creators, sports, AI, or communication, we should talk.

CTA:

- Build with MAGSNAP.
- Partner on a module.

### 10. Final CTA

Two CTAs:

- Join Founding 900.
- Become a Module Partner.

## Tone

The tone should be:

- Founder-led.
- Technical but human.
- Confident but not overclaiming.
- Platform-minded.
- Safety-aware.

Avoid:

- “Smart glasses.”
- “AI glasses.”
- “100% safe.”
- “Never falls off.”
- “Anything you like.”
- “Free headlight.”
- “Military / tactical” as first message.

## Recommended English Copy Blocks

### Hero

> Not smart glasses.  
> A wearable magnetic platform.
>
> Camera in the morning. Light at night. One base platform. Snap on what you need.

### WME Explainer

> MAGSNAP is a lightweight eyewear base with a standardized magnetic interface for snap-on modules. The first modules are camera and light. The long-term vision is a wearable magnetic ecosystem for creators, athletes, travelers, and future AI devices.

### Founder Proof

> I have worn MAGSNAP for over a year. It is not a concept render or a gadget I only take out for demos. It is the base platform I actually wear.

### Safety FAQ

> MAGSNAP does not place a prism, display, or lens in front of your eyes, and you do not look at the camera while using it. The camera is designed to sit outside your normal line of sight. If you experience eye strain, double vision, headache, dizziness, or blocked vision, stop using it immediately.
